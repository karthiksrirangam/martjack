Add/Edit Bundle Product

Bundle Product literally means the combination of various existing of your Webstore in to a single product. Assembled Desktop,Party wear,gift basket are some examples of a Bundle Product. You can create different groups map suitable products to them, which can be selected by end-users while buying customized version of Bundle Product. Price and weight of the bundle product can be configured either fixed or based on the selected items(i.e.dynamic).
