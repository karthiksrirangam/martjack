Manage Product Categories

Category Management is taxonomy of arraning Product in a hierarchy. The `Category Management` module has been completely revamped to a tree-structured(hierarchical) user interface(UI) to provide look & feel, quick loading and easy navigation. Now it's much easier to create and manage the Categories, Sub-categories and attributes. You can directly edit the name of a 'Category' or 'Ref.code' by just double clicking the respective values and also find the product count against respective Categories. A search box also provided for finding required Categories/Sub-Categories quickly.
